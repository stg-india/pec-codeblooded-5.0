About Us

Welcome to our H1B Data Analysis Platform! We are passionate about leveraging data to gain insights and make informed decisions. Our platform focuses on H1B visa data from the years 2016 to 2019, aiming to provide valuable statistical information and analysis to better understand trends and patterns in foreign labor performance.

Our Objectives

Data Ingestion and Storage

We ensure seamless ingestion of H1B data into a PostgreSQL database, enabling efficient storage and retrieval for analysis.

Data Analysis and Insights

Our team is dedicated to deriving meaningful insights from the data. We use SQL queries and custom Python functions to calculate essential statistical measures like mean, median, and percentiles.

API for Accessible Insights

We've built a Django-based API that offers easy access to key metrics such as the number of records, mean salary, median salary, and percentile salaries. Our aim is to make this data readily available for users to empower their decision-making processes.
